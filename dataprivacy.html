Data Privacy

Our company is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that any data you provide us is kept secure. We take our responsibility to protect your personal information seriously, and we have put in place appropriate measures to safeguard your data.

What information we collect

We only collect the health data that you choose to provide us through our web application. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, address, or other contact information. We also do not use any cookies or other tracking technologies that may identify you or your device.

How we use your information

We use the data you provide to us to improve our product and provide insights that may be beneficial to you or others. We will not share or sell your data to any third parties, and we will only use it for the purpose it was intended.

How to protect your identity

We encourage you to use an email address that is not linked to your identity when you interact with our web application. This will help to further protect your privacy and ensure that your data cannot be linked back to you. We also want to assure you that our web application is designed to run in a double-blinded way, which means that we will not know your identity, and you will not know who we are.

Changes to our privacy policy

We may make changes to our privacy policy from time to time, and any updates will be posted on this page. We encourage you to check this page regularly to ensure that you are aware of our most current policies.

If you have any questions or concerns about our data privacy policies, please contact us using the information provided on our Contact Us page.
